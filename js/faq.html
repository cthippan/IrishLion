Directions

From the North:

  • Travel South on Hwy 37 to Bloomington.
  • Take the 46 East Bloomington Exit.
  • Turn Right (South) onto College Avenue.
  • At the Courthouse Square Turn Right onto West Kirkwood Avenue (5th Street).
  • The Irish Lion is a 1/2 block west of the Courthouse Square on the right.
﻿

From the South:

  • Travel North on Hwy 37 to the Bloomington East 3rd Street Exit.
  • Turn Right (East) onto Third Street. East Third will flow into/become West Kirkwood/5th Street.
  • Continue East on Kirkwood, over the B-Line Trail (Morton Avenue).
  • The Irish Lion is on the lefthand side 1/2 a block before the Courthouse Square.
    • ﻿

From the East:

  • Travel West on Hwy 46 to Bloomington.
  • Continue on Hwy 46 which becomes, East 3rd Street at the College Mall Intersection.
  • Follow East Third Street through the IU campus to downtown.
  • Turn right (North) onto Walnut Street staying in the far left lane.
  • At the Courthouse Square 5th Street/Kirkwood Avenue turn left, traveling west.
  • The Irish Lion is a ½ block west of the Courthouse Square, on the right.

From the West:

  • From West Hwy 45 or West Hwy 48: turn left (North) onto Hwy 37 and follow directions above for "From the South"
  • Travel East on Hwy 46 to Bloomington.
  • Take the 46 East-Bloomington Exit.
  • Turn Right (South) onto College Avenue.
  • At the Courthouse Square Turn Right onto West Kirkwood Avenue (5th Street).
  • The Irish Lion is a 1/2 block west of the Courthouse Square on your right.
